Our Story

Welcome to Taste Of Terai, your destination for authentic traditional sweets from the Terai region of Nepal. We bring you the true taste of homemade Mithai crafted with age-old recipes and premium ingredients.

Our journey began with a simple mission - to preserve and share the rich culinary heritage of Terai with the world. Every piece of sweet we create carries the warmth of tradition and the sweetness of memories.
