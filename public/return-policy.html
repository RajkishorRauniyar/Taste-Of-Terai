Return & Refund Policy

At Taste Of Terai, we are committed to ensuring customer satisfaction. Since we deal with food items, we have specific guidelines for returns and refunds.

Return Window

We offer a 2-3 day return window from the date of delivery for damaged or defective items. Please inspect your order upon delivery and contact us immediately if the item is defective, damaged, or if you receive the wrong item.

Eligible Returns

  • Damaged or spoiled products
  • Wrong item delivered (different from what was ordered)
  • Missing items from order
  • Products with quality issues

Non-Returnable Items

  • Perishable food items (once delivered and accepted)
  • Products opened or used
  • Items returned after the 3-day window
  • Items damaged due to customer mishandling

How to Request a Return

  1. Contact us within 2-3 days of delivery at +977-9765151023 or +977-9845510611
  2. Provide your order ID and describe the issue
  3. Share photos of the damaged/defective product
  4. Our team will review your request within 24-48 hours
  5. Once approved, we will arrange for pickup or refund

Refund Process

Once your return is approved, refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days through the original payment method. For bank transfers, please provide your bank account details.

Exchange Policy

If you prefer an exchange instead of a refund, we will replace the damaged item with a fresh one at no additional cost. The exchange will be shipped once we receive and verify the returned item.

Contact Us

For any return-related queries, please contact us:

  • Email: info@tasteofterai.com
  • WhatsApp: +977-9765151023
