Shipping & Delivery Policy

At Taste Of Terai, we strive to deliver your favorite sweets fresh and on time across Nepal. Please read our shipping policy below.

Delivery Areas

We deliver to the following locations:

  • Kathmandu Valley (Kalanki, Bojepokhari, and surrounding areas)
  • Lalitpur
  • Bhaktapur
  • Birgunj
  • Hetauda
  • Other major cities in Nepal (delivery time may vary)

Delivery Time

Standard delivery time is 3-5 business days from the date of order confirmation. For Kathmandu Valley, delivery may be completed within 1-3 days.

Delivery Time by Location

  • Kathmandu Valley: 1-3 days
  • Birgunj: 2-3 days
  • Hetauda: 2-4 days
  • Other Cities: 3-5 days

Delivery Charges

  • Inside Kathmandu Valley: Rs. 80 per order
  • Outside Kathmandu Valley: Rs. 100-150 per order (depending on location)
  • Free delivery for orders above Rs. 2000

Order Processing Time

Orders are processed within 24-48 hours after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

Delivery Partners

We work with reliable courier services to ensure safe and timely delivery of your products. Our delivery partners include:

  • Gyane Courier
  • Siddhartha Courier
  • Other verified courier services

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via SMS. You can track your order using our Track Order page.

Delivery Instructions

  • Please provide complete delivery address including landmark
  • Ensure someone is available to receive the delivery
  • Provide a working phone number for delivery coordination
  • For office deliveries, mention company name and floor

Delivery Updates

You will receive SMS updates at each stage:

  • Order confirmed
  • Order shipped
  • Out for delivery
  • Delivered
