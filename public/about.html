Our Story

Welcome to Taste Of Terai, your premier online destination for authentic Mithai and traditional sweets from the beautiful Terai region of Nepal. We believe that every sweet tells a story—a story of tradition, love, and the rich cultural heritage that makes our nation truly special.

Our journey began with a heartfelt mission: to bring the authentic flavors of Terai directly to your doorstep. We understand that Mithai is not just a dessert—it's a symbol of celebration, togetherness, and the sweet moments we share with our loved ones. That's why we are committed to preserving and sharing these time-honored recipes with every generation.

As a trusted and modern online marketplace for Nepal, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern convenience. We take pride in offering quality products at competitive prices, ensuring that every family can experience the joy of authentic Mithai without compromise. Our commitment to transparency means you always know exactly what you're getting—pure ingredients, genuine flavors, and unmatched freshness.

What sets us apart is our dedication to customer satisfaction and fast delivery across Nepal. We believe that distance should never diminish the taste of home. Whether you're celebrating a festival, marking a special occasion, or simply craving the sweets of your childhood, Taste Of Terai is here to deliver happiness to your door.
